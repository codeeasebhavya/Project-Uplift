📊 Global Poverty: The Hard Truth

  • 700+ million people live on less than $2.15/day (World Bank, 2023).
  • 1 in 10 people globally lives in extreme poverty.
  • Sub-Saharan Africa accounts for ~60% of the world's poor.
  • COVID-19 pushed ~100 million people back into poverty.

🇮🇳 India: Progress with Challenges

  • ~13.5 crore Indians lifted out of poverty between 2015–2021 (NITI Aayog).
  • Bihar, Jharkhand, Uttar Pradesh have highest poverty ratios.
  • Multidimensional Poverty Index (MPI) considers health, education, living standards—not just income.

📈 Key Indicators

  • 👶 Child Poverty Rate: ~17.5% globally (UNICEF)
  • 💧 People without clean water: 2.2 billion
  • 📚 Out-of-school children: 244 million (UNESCO)

🌐 Why This Matters

Poverty limits access to basic needs—education, healthcare, food, dignity. Eradicating poverty builds resilience, equity, and peace across the globe.
